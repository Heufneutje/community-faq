Getting Started with the Desert Bus IRC Chat Room

Welcome to the Desert Bus IRC room! You're in a very exciting place. IRC, however, can be daunting to newcomers, so we're here to help get you set up and allow you to have the most enjoyable Desert Bus experience possible!

In order for us to be able to give you information most appropriately, you must choose your path:

  • I've never heard of IRC before! I want something that's already set up and lets me get right to the chatting and/or bidding.
  • I have heard of IRC before, but I still prefer something that's already set up.
  • I use or would like to get my own IRC client. What settings do I need to use your network?

Or ask a question:

  • Why should I have a Desert Bus Donor account?
  • How do I bid in a live auction?
