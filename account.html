Desert Bus Donor Accounts

A donor account affords you many opportunities, including glorious fame and fortune!
OK, so they're not that great, but there are still benefits you get with a donor account that you can't get without:

  • Your donations can be tracked to your account, so if you win a giveaway, for example, it's much easier to find the person who won and send the prize right to them.
  • You can't bid in an auction (either type—live or silent!) without one! Many of the best prizes each year go up for auction. Make sure you at least have a shot— sign up for a donor account! (If you want some of the more popular prizes, it helps if you're the richest person ever.)
  • Recognition as a donor: you get voiced in the IRC chat room.

Signing up for a donor account is easy. All we ask is that you give us your email address and a password you will use to access your donor account. That's it! We don't require any further information from you for the account itself. We only require any further information on an as-needed basis. (For example, we'll ask you to enter your address if we need to ship you something, of course.)
