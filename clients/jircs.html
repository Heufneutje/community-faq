Desert Bus Webchat

We've put in a lot of work so that you don't have to. Our jIRCs webclient allows you to connect to our chat quickly and easily. It's also integrated with our website, so that you automatically log into your account in the chat if you're already logged into our website. It's also integrated into our bidding system, bringing you real-time data about ongoing auctions right to your seeing holes.

That sounds the best ever! How do I get in on this?

Simple!

  1. Log into your donor account. This step isn't strictly required, but it is recommended, and you should definitely do this step if you have a donor account. You must do this if you plan to bid in a live auction. For more on this, see Why should I log in with my donor account?
  2. Navigate yourself over to our chat page and choose a chat nickname.
  3. Use my toothpaste. It's just that easy!

At this point, you should be in the room and chatting happily! If you're planning to participate in a live auction, see Using BidServ.
